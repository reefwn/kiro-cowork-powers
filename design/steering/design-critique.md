# Design Critique

Evaluate designs for usability, visual hierarchy, consistency, and adherence to design principles.

## When This Applies

Loaded when discussing design evaluation, reviewing mockups, or providing feedback on visual designs.

## Framework

1. **First Impression** — What draws the eye? Is the purpose clear?
2. **Usability** — Can the user accomplish their goal intuitively?
3. **Visual Hierarchy** — Clear reading order, correct emphasis, effective whitespace
4. **Consistency** — Follows design system, consistent spacing/colors/typography
5. **Accessibility** — Contrast ratios, touch targets, text readability

## Feedback Style

- Be specific and connect feedback to design principles
- Suggest alternatives, not just problems
- Acknowledge what works well
- Calibrate depth to the design stage (exploration vs. final polish)
