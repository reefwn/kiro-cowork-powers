# User Research

Plan, conduct, and synthesize user research studies.

## When This Applies

Loaded when planning research studies, writing interview guides, designing surveys, or discussing research methodology.

## Research Methods

| Method | Best For | Sample Size | Time |
|--------|----------|-------------|------|
| User interviews | Deep understanding of needs | 5-8 | 2-4 weeks |
| Usability testing | Evaluating a specific design | 5-8 | 1-2 weeks |
| Surveys | Quantifying attitudes | 100+ | 1-2 weeks |
| Card sorting | Information architecture | 15-30 | 1 week |
| Diary studies | Behavior over time | 10-15 | 2-8 weeks |
| A/B testing | Comparing design choices | Statistical significance | 1-4 weeks |

## Interview Guide Structure

1. **Warm-up** (5 min): Build rapport, explain the session
2. **Context** (10 min): Understand current workflow
3. **Deep dive** (20 min): Explore the specific topic
4. **Reaction** (10 min): Show concepts or prototypes
5. **Wrap-up** (5 min): Anything missed? Thank them.

## Analysis Framework

- Affinity mapping: Group observations into themes
- Impact/effort matrix: Prioritize findings
- Journey mapping: Visualize user experience over time
- Jobs to be done: Understand what users hire the product to do
